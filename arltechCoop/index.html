What Makes Us Tick!

At arltechCoop, we believe that we shouldn't give up our commitment to democratic participation when we enter our places of work, so we are committed to developing worker coops that operate on the principle of one worker, one vote.

Our Mission

arltechCoop is a cooperative of cooperatives made up of people working together to create just, fulfilling, and meaningful work for members in the spirit of social solidarity.

Technology

The solidarity economy we seek requires that the tools we use to design and build our lived world together be owned by all of us. All the software we use is therefore free software, and we likewise use designs for hardware that are open and collaborative.
