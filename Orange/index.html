About Oranges

Why People Love Orange!

Orange is simply the best color there is. It is warm, sunny, and bright. Associated with amusement, the unconventional, warmth, fire, energy, activity, taste and aroma, it is an important symbolic color in Buddism and Hinduism. Orange makes you feel happy. It is the color of pumkins and Halloween.

Our Mission

Our mission is to share our love of the color orange. Here you'll find links to all kinds of things that celebrate both the amazing color, and the sweet, delicious fruit from which the color gets its name.

Not Just a Great Color

Before is was a color, orange was a fruit, a sweet, delicious fruit high in vitamin C and ready to be squeezed into a refreshing breakfast drink.
